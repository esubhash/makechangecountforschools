About us ...

Inspired from San Diego downtown program called “Make Change Count” to raise money for homeless people, We have started a club called Make Change Count for Schools (MCCFS), through which we hope to create a funding platform for local schools through active community participation that is both convenient and effective. Our club with the help from San Diego based parking technology company called IPS Group Inc, installs a donation meter infront of schools, essentially a repurposed parking meter. Students and parents can use the meter to donate spare change as well as make other donations to the school via credit card. We hope our program will provide creative funding options for local schools by involving communities for mutual benefit.
