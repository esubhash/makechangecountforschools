How it works

Program funding

Make change count for schools collects and organizes initial funding from sponsors

Installation

With the help of IPS Group Inc, MCCFS installs donation meters in a friendly, convenient location to help schools raise money.

Donation

Parents, students, and other people visiting schools can easily donate to schools via the donation meter. Donations can come in the form of either spare change or a credit-card transaction.

Donation receipt

At the end of the fiscal year, patrons may view an electronic copy of their 501(c)(3) donations at https://receipt.ipsmetersystems.com/

What We Do

Identify Schools

We identify schools to pitch the program and value proposition to the School (PTSA board).

Installation

With the help of IPS Group Inc, MCCFS installs donation meters in a friendly, convenient location to help schools raise money.

Setup

We will coordinate with the IPS Group Inc. to setup

  • Donation meter account
  • Data management system account
  • School's merchant gateway account
  • Receipt printing account

Training school staff

We will coordinate with the IPS Group Inc. to train school staff in:

  • IPS's Data mangement system to monitor revenue reports
  • Collect coings from donation meter
  • Receipt system

Launch

Coordinate with school (PTSA), IPS, and local media to schedule a launch date

  • IPS's Data mangement system to monitor revenue reports
  • Collect coings from donation meter
  • Receipt system

Post launch

We will coordinate with the IPS Group Inc. to train school staff in:

  • Schedule any necessary maintenance checks
  • Act as a liaison between IPS and school to address any meter or system related issues
  • Help schools effectively leverage the donation meter for raising money
